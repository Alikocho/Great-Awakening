Something vast and ancient beyond comprehension stirs beneath R'lyeh. Its followers — dormant for millennia — move openly now through the streets of New York, Shanghai, Moscow. World governments have 72 hours before full manifestation becomes irreversible.


Each power must decide: What do you save? What do you sacrifice? And who will lead humanity into whatever comes next?

HOW TO PLAY

🎯 OBJECTIVE Survive the Great Awakening. Maximize your nation's Stability, minimize global Madness, and contain Cthulhu's reach. Each power has unique goals — cooperation and betrayal both serve.
⚡ EACH TURN Read the situation briefing. Choose an action type. Write your orders in plain language. The AI Oracle adjudicates outcomes, updates world state, and narrates consequences for all powers.
🏆 VICTORY The game ends after 4 turns. Two verdicts are delivered: a global outcome based on whether the Awakening was contained, and individual rankings reflecting how well each power advanced its interests under pressure. The Oracle evaluates both throughout the session. Rankings run from Dominant to Struggling — and a power can rank first in a world that fell.
